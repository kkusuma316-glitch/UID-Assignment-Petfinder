How Old Is A Dog In Human Years?

Dog years are multiplied by seven to get a human age, as a dog's lifespan is one seventh of a person's. Converting dog years to human years depends on various factors such as size, breed, and health.

Experts agree that the first two years of a dog's life are similar to a human teenager's development. Smaller dogs generally live longer than larger breeds.

Factors That Affect Dog Age

  • Breed and genetics
  • Size and weight
  • Exercise and diet
  • Regular vet checkups
  • Daily activity level
  • Overall lifestyle and environment
