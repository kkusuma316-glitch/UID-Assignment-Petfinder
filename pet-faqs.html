Pet-Adoption FAQs

Thank you for using Petfinder! We’re here to help you every step of the way from just looking to adopting your perfect pet. This FAQ page answers some of the most commonly asked questions about adoption and caring for pets.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Do I Search For A Pet?

Use the search bar to find pets based on location, breed, size, and age. Browse available shelters and rescues near you.

Can I Save My Favorite Pets?

Yes! You can create an account and save pets to your favorites list so you can revisit them later.

How Often Are Listings Updated?

Pet listings are updated regularly by shelters and rescue groups to ensure information stays accurate.

What Should I Prepare Before Adoption?

Prepare food, toys, bedding, vet information and a safe environment before bringing your new pet home.

Helpful Adoption Tips

Safe Home

Create a safe and comfortable space for your pet to adjust easily.

Healthy Food

Provide balanced nutrition and maintain a proper feeding schedule.

Love & Care

Patience and affection help pets settle into their new family.
